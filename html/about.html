About The Quirky Studio

Meet Our Founder


Meet Cheslyn, the founder behind The Quirky Studio, a captivating costume store that brings fantasy to life. From a young age, Cheslyn was enamored with the captivating worlds of anime, superhero movies and horrors.Cheslyn transformed his lifelong love into a thriving business, curating a treasure trove of meticulously crafted costumes.
